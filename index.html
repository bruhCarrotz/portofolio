About

Hi, I’m Anastasia. I work in digital payments as an Associate Consultant at Fime, part of the Consult Hyperion team. My focus is on EMV systems, certification, and implementation across contact and contactless technologies. I enjoy understanding how systems behave in practice and making sure they work reliably.

I’m interested in the details behind how payments actually function, from certification processes to tokenization and digital wallets. With a background in computer science and experience across both edtech and fintech, I like working at the intersection of systems, processes, and people.
